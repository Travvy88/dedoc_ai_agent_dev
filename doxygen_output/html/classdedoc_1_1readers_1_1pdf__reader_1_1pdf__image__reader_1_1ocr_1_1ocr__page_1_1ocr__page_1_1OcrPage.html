Detailed Description

Represents OCR results from the Tesseract.

You may see the description in https://www.tomrochette.com/tesseract-tsv-format

Output of the Tesseract has a hierarchical structure: Pages (level 1) are divided on Blocks (level 2), Blocks are divided on Paragraph (level 3), Paragraphs are divided on Lines (level 4) Lines are divided on Words (level 5) Originally only words have text content, but we will extract text from the lower level
