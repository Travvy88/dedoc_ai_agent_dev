function's steps: 1) detects Houph lines for detecting rotate angle.

Then input image has rotated on the rotate angle. 2) performs morphological operations under rotated images for detecting horizontal and vertical lines 3) on found lines detects tree contours with help cv2.findContours :param img: input image :param config: dict from config.py :return: contours, contours's hierarchy, rotated image, rotate angle
