Detailed Description

\par \b Purpose\/ \: To convert binary blob files (mime=application/octet-stream) that carry image-like extensions (.bmp, .jpg, .tiff, etc.) into PNG by delegating to PNGConverter.

Converts image-like documents with mime=application/octet-stream into PNG. Look to the :class:~dedoc.converters.AbstractConverter documentation to get the information about the methods' parameters.
