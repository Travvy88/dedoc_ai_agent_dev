Press Release on the official visit to Indonesia by Minister of Foreign Affairs Bui Thanh Son

(MOFA) - At the invitation of Indonesian Minister of Foreign Affairs Retno Marsudi, Minister of Foreign Affairs Bui Thanh Son will pay an official visit to Indonesia and co-chair the 4th meeting of the Viet Nam – Indonesia Bilateral Cooperation Committee in Jakarta, Indonesia from 19 to 21 July 2022.
